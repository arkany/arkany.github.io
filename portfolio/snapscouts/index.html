SnapScouts is agitprop software theatre. SnapScouts received over 80,000 hits was mentioned in several blogs including Wired, LA Times, Reddit and Download Squad (links below). The Android application and accompanying site lets you download the app to your Android phone. The app ultimately promotes kids spying on your neighborhood and parents. This is, of course, a terrible idea, but by creating awareness, it will hopefully promote privacy.
