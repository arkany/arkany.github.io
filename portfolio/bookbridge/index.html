BookBridge

BookBridge is my thesis project the ITP Masterâ€™s Program at NYU. It is the end product of my research into the digital transition for literature. I investigated the current state of digital books through interviews and research, exposing their benefits and limitations. I examined the problems libraries face including budget cuts, fair use, and digital rights management. Several existing book sharing communities are explored, both legal and illegal. I built my prototype, which can be found at bookbridge.me, to test my thesis and offered around 100 books to users for testing and analysis.
